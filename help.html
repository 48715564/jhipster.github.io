Help and bugs

If you want help:

  • you can contact JHipster directly on Twitter: @java_hipster
  • you can post your question on Stackoverflow with the "yeoman-generator" tag - but still please do warn @java_hipster on Twitter, just to be sure we don't miss your question!

If you found a bug, please fill a ticket on our bug tracker, we'll be happy to resolve your issue!
